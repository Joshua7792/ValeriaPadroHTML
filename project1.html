The New Ceviche Menu Design

Project Overview

The New Ceviche Menu Design project involved creating a visually appealing and functionally organized menu for an authentic Peruvian cuisine restaurant. Each section is clearly distinguished, offering patrons a delightful selection from appetizers to desserts and beverages, set against a visually stimulating backdrop that evokes the vibrancy of Peruvian culture.
